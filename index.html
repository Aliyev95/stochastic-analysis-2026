General Information

About the Conference

The V.I. Romanovskiy Institute of Mathematics of the Academy of Sciences of the Republic of Uzbekistan, in cooperation with the National University of Uzbekistan named after Mirzo Ulugbek, will hold an international scientific conference entitled 'Actual Problems of Stochastic Analysis' on May 12-13, 2026, dedicated to the 106th anniversary of academician S.H.Sirojiddinov and the 85th anniversary of academician Sh.Q.Formanov.

The conference is being held with the purpose of expanding scientific cooperation and communication among scientists from different countries, familiarizing with and discussing new and important scientific results obtained in probability theory, mathematical statistics, random dynamical systems, and, at the same time, other areas of mathematics, and discussing problems that are currently urgent to solve.

Abstract Submission Deadline

Requirements:

Language

Abstracts must be written in English

Length

Abstract should not exceed 2 pages (A4 format, 12pt font)

Format

TEX (LaTeX) and PDF format

Structure

Download the official TEX template for preparing your abstract and follow the provided instructions
